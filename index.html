— WHY THIS BLOG EXISTS

Informed Students.
Responsible Citizens.
A Greener Future.

This blog is a student-led academic project by Information Technology students, integrating environmental literacy with digital communication skills. Each post addresses a real and pressing environmental issue — from the root causes of global warming to its climate impacts and the devastating effects of biodiversity loss.

BLOG OBJECTIVES
🌐
Explain Environmental Issues

Present important environmental challenges and their effects on society, ecosystems, and communities in the Philippines.

🖥️
Digital Communication

Apply web and blogging skills to organize, design, and publish informative content using a modern digital platform.

💡
Raise Awareness

Promote environmental literacy, responsibility, and a sense of stewardship toward our natural world and resources.

📖
Academic Excellence

Demonstrate research skills, accurate sourcing, and the ability to present complex topics clearly and engagingly.
